Transportation & The 15-Minute City

The Evolution of Mobility

In the mid-20th century, cities were designed for cars. Today, planners are reversing this trend to focus on Active Transport (walking and cycling).

Key Fact:

Public transport carries 10x more people per square meter of road space than private cars.

Three Main Types of Transit

  • BRT (Bus Rapid Transit): High-capacity buses with their own dedicated lanes.
  • Light Rail: Electric trains that run on city streets.
  • Micromobility: E-scooters and bike-sharing systems.
