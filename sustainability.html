Sustainable Urbanism

The Urban Heat Island Effect

Cities are often 1-7°C warmer than surrounding rural areas due to concrete and asphalt absorbing heat. Urban planners combat this with "Green Infrastructure."

Did You Know?

A single mature tree can provide the same cooling effect as 10 room-sized air conditioners operating 20 hours a day.

Essential Green Tools

  1. Permeable Pavement: Surfaces that allow rainwater to soak through, preventing floods.
  2. Vertical Gardens: Growing plants on the sides of skyscrapers to improve air quality.
  3. Urban Canopy: Strategic tree planting to provide shade and habitat.
